Nice to meet you! My name is Jasfiq Rahman.

Why I got into game development and why it excites me so much can be summed up in one sentence: I love playing God!

Before diving into game development, my initial dream was to become a novelist. What connects my passion for writing with game development is the joy of bringing entire worlds to life through my fingertips. This profound sense of creative fulfilment is something I value deeply, and I can easily envision myself immersed in this pursuit for years to come.

My educational journey has been both compelling and dynamic. While enrolled in a university, I pursued a degree in computer science; however, the curriculum lacked any focus on game development. Consequently, I took it upon myself to acquire the necessary knowledge, leveraging the abundance of resources available for self-learning.

Balancing coursework and independent learning proved to be a challenging yet rewarding endeavour. Through dedicated effort, I successfully attained a level of proficiency that enabled me to assume various roles within the industry. Presently, I am enrolled in a Master’s program specializing in game development, a significant milestone that brings me immense joy. This formal education has not only provided a structured foundation but has also unveiled numerous avenues for exploration within my chosen field.

The continual pursuit of knowledge is a passion I hold dear. The Master’s program represents a pivotal step in my journey, opening doors to exciting new opportunities that demand my dedication and enthusiasm. I aspire to establish a foothold in the industry, actively striving to augment my profile with additional projects and practical experience.

I am fascinated by the intricate components that converge to create a cohesive game experience, encompassing gameplay dynamics, user interface design, NPC and enemy artificial intelligences, as well as the overall maintenance and system design. Proficient in both Unity and the Unreal Engine, I have cultivated a strong command of C# and C++, alongside a proficiency in Unreal's Blueprints.

My expertise spans various domains, including optimization, debugging, networking, testing, prototyping, and more. Additionally, I am well-versed in version control practices, employing Git for the management of all my projects, both personal and professional.

While I also have an interest in exploring game design, my current emphasis is exclusively on the development aspect. I am dedicated to enhancing my skills and refining my instincts within the development domain before incorporating game design into my skill set in the future.
